About

First, we appreciate your decision to be part of us. 100 Pages is an all-poetry platform that seeks to get the most out of not just creative but unique artistic mind. We're looking to build a platform where every poet can call it a home. Note that this new home is currently under active construction, and we let you in so you can provide with valuable feedback that can help us make it better. We believe in you. That's why down the line we'll be introducing poetry competitions where you can pair with friends to write for real money. It all starts today, and it starts with you!

At 100Pages, we believe in the power of poetry to transcend boundaries, to evoke empathy, and to connect souls. Our vision is to create a space where poets, both seasoned and emerging, can share their stories, reflections, and perspectives with the world. We aspire to be a haven for those who seek solace, inspiration, and a shared understanding through the beauty of language. You can get started by signing up here. If you're already signed up and has some valuable feedback for us, head over to the feedback section. For being part of this great journey, asante.
